What Atithi-Setu Inventory does — in plain language

Auto-deducts on every order

Configure recipes once. After that, every customer order silently subtracts the right amount from your stock. 50 butter chickens → 10 kg chicken automatically gone.

Tells you what to buy & when

"Paneer — 2.3 days cover left, suggest 8 kg from Hari Dairy." One click raises a PO PDF, e-mailed to the supplier. Morning ping: arrives today.

Catches what you can't see

15-min Sunday count on your phone. System tells you exactly how much went missing this week, in ₹. Theft, spoilage, over-portioning have nowhere to hide.

What makes it different from spreadsheets

  • Zero double-entry. Recipe configured once, every order auto-deducts. The cashier doesn't know inventory exists.
  • Mobile-first physical counts. Walk the walk-in cooler with one hand on the phone — big ± steppers, no laptop, no Excel.
  • GST-aware end-to-end. Every PO captures GST; every supplier price is a real bill, not a guess. Year-end audit ready.
  • One source of truth. Append-only audit log. Every gram traceable: who, when, why, against which order or GRN.
  • Customisable. Festival multipliers (Diwali ×2 paneer), reorder cadence, supplier lead times, notification wording, storage locations.
