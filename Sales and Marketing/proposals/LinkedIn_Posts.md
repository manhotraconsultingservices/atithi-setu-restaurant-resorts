- Put the PDF link in the **first comment**, not the post body (LinkedIn buries posts with outbound links)
- Reply to every comment within 60 minutes for the first 4 hours
- Use 3-5 hashtags maximum (more = demoted)
- Tag genuine connections only (1-2 max per post)
- Add a UTM-tagged URL so you can track conversion: `?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=2026q3_<segment>`

❌ **DON'T**
- Cross-post the same text to X/Facebook the same day (LinkedIn detects and demotes)
- Add more than one URL in the post body
- Post on Saturday or Sunday (B2B engagement crashes)
- Use clickbait headlines ("You won't believe...") — LinkedIn demotes these
- Edit the post within the first 60 minutes (resets algorithm distribution)

# Tips for the founder's voice

The posts read better in first person — "I spent 2025 working with 12 cafés" — than third person — "Atithi-Setu worked with 12 cafés." LinkedIn rewards personal voice.

Don't be afraid to admit limitations:
> "The lowest pilot saving was ₹4.2L. Not every café will get ₹5L+. But every one of them got past ₹4L."

Specificity > superlatives. "12 cafés in 4 cities" beats "many customers." "₹42,000/month" beats "significant savings."
