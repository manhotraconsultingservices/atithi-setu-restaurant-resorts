Story #3 — Compliance

Folios, GST, Form-C —
compliance on autopilot.

Auto-folio at check-in

Folios open automatically when a guest checks in. Per-night room rates post nightly. Service charges flow in as they complete. GST is calculated state-aware: CGST + SGST for intra-state guests, IGST for inter-state. No spreadsheet at checkout.

Form-C for foreign guests

For foreign nationals, Form-C is auto-generated as a PDF. Submit to FRRO directly. Compliance log keeps an audit trail of every form filed — no penalty surprises.
